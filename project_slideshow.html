Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation I wanted to look at the fuel efficiency of vehicles against various variables such as year(To get a view of how fuel efficiency has improved over a certain time period), engine size (To see whether the size of the engine affects the fuel efficiency of the vehicle) and Vehicle Class( To know which class of vehicles are much more fuel efficient)

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset consists of 3929 entries and 20 columns. Variables such as engine size, year and Vehicle Class are compared against combined fuel efficiency to look at fuel efficiency in the periods between 2013 to 2018.

Conclusion¶

Combined Fuel Efficiency is influeced by a myriad of factors such as engine size , Vehicle Class and co2 emissions. This helps identify areas to consider when loking at fuel efficiency. The data visualized shows that larger engines size in vehicles reduces fuel efficiency, it also shows how increased co2 emissions contribute to reduced fuel efficiency and that fuel efficiency is much more likely to be observed in smaller vehicles than larger ones. All vehicles classes have been seen to equally cntribute to fuel efficiency.
